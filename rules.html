1. Professional Conduct

  • Maintain a respectful and professional tone at all times.
  • Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or unprofessional behavior.
  • Treat every member, regardless of rank, with respect and courtesy.

2. Confidentiality and Privacy

  • Do not share sensitive or confidential information related to operations, clients, or team members.
  • Personal information of members must not be shared without consent.

3. Relevant Discussions

  • Keep discussions on-topic and related to private security, company operations, or relevant training.
  • Use designated channels for off-topic conversations, if available.

4. Communication Etiquette

  • Use clear and concise language to ensure effective communication.
  • Avoid spamming messages, excessive tagging, or unnecessary clutter.

5. Rank and Role Respect

  • Acknowledge and respect the hierarchy of ranks and roles within the server.
  • Follow instructions from moderators, administrators, and team leaders.

6. Media and File Sharing

  • Share only approved documents, images, or videos related to security topics or training.
  • Ensure that all shared materials are virus-free and appropriate for the serverâ€™s audience.

7. Code of Ethics

  • Uphold the principles of integrity, reliability, and accountability.
  • Misrepresentation of yourself or the company is strictly prohibited.

8. Participation and Activity

  • Be active and contribute to discussions, training sessions, or other server activities.
  • Inform moderators or admins if you will be inactive for an extended period.

9. Prohibited Content

  • No discussions or sharing of illegal activities, unauthorized weapon use, or unethical practices.
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or any form of harassment will result in immediate removal.

10. Reporting Issues

  • Report any rule violations, suspicious behavior, or technical issues to the moderators or admins.
  • Use the designated channels or direct messages to communicate such concerns.
